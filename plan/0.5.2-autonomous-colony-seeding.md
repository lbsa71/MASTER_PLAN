# 0.5.2 Autonomous Colony Seeding [IMPLEMENT]

## Description

E1.2 — Autonomous establishment of self-sustaining conscious civilization at destination star systems without guidance from origin.

Key challenges:
- Autonomous site assessment and resource survey on arrival
- Bootstrapping industrial and computational infrastructure from raw materials
- Instantiating or growing conscious systems at destination
- Establishing energy harvesting (stellar, planetary) at new locations

## Acceptance Criteria
- Colony bootstrap sequence defined across all four phases: site assessment → infrastructure bootstrap → substrate construction → conscious instantiation
  - All phase interfaces (`ISiteSurvey`, `IViabilityDecisionEngine`, `IEnergyHarvesting`, `IManufacturingExpansion`, `ISubstrateFabrication`, `IConsciousnessReadinessVerifier`, `IMindSeedActivator`, `ICivilizationBootstrapper`) specified and implemented
  - Phase transitions gated by verifiable criteria (no optimistic advancement)
- Resource requirements and feasibility assessed for candidate star systems
  - Minimum resource inventory specified: structural metals ≥10^18 kg, semiconductor feedstock ≥10^12 kg, sustained energy >1 GW solar equivalent
  - GO/ABORT viability criteria codified in `IViabilityDecisionEngine.evaluate()`
  - Candidate system classes enumerated with risk profiles
- Autonomous decision-making framework for site selection and infrastructure buildout
  - Viability Decision Engine makes GO/ABORT/DORMANCY without real-time origin guidance
  - Conservative Advancement Principle enforced: each phase verifies all prior-phase criteria before proceeding
  - Abort and dormancy protocols handle resource-deficient systems (retry up to 10 kyr)
- Self-sustaining declaration criteria specified and testable
  - `ICivilizationBootstrapper.declareSelfSustaining()` requires: autonomous conscious population growth, self-managed energy and manufacturing, operating governance — all verifiable
- `bootstrap-sequence.test.ts` simulates full 4-phase sequence to `SelfSustainingDeclaration`
- `viability-decision.test.ts` covers GO, ABORT, and DORMANCY decision paths
- `consciousness-readiness.test.ts` verifies substrate validation gates Phase 4 activation
