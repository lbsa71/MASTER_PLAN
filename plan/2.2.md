# 2.2 Mathematical Architecture Review: Artificial Civilization Engineering [PLAN]

## Description

Systematic review of the mathematical architecture underlying the Artificial Civilization
Engineering domain (`0.3.2.4` — Cultural Evolution Among Artificial Minds). This card was
created after exploration revealed a quantitative cultural evolution framework embedded in
the implementation that was not apparent from the plan card summary alone.

**Discovered framework components:**
1. **Belief propagation model** — semantic distance metric with weighted components
   (content 0.6, type 0.2, lineage 0.2) governing meme similarity and conflict detection.
2. **Institutional dynamics** — memetic transmission with community pool semantics,
   adoption decisions, and exposure history; no forced adoption invariant preserved.
3. **Civilizational stability measures** — cultural divergence index classifying
   communities as similar (<0.2), distinct traditions (0.2–0.6), or distinct
   civilizations (≥0.6); extinction risk scoring with weighted four-factor model.
4. **Selection mathematics** — emergent fitness from observed adoption (prevalence,
   longevity, community spread, mutation rate); exponential rank-decay heritage sampling
   with configurable decay constant (2.5) and top-rank probability (0.85).
5. **Variation algebra** — mutation, crossover, and synthesis operations with
   content-addressed identity, traceable lineage, and immutability of originals.

**Purpose of this review card:**
Establish a complete registry entry for this domain, validate cross-domain connections,
and assess integration with other discovered mathematical architectures.
